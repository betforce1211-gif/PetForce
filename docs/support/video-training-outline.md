## Video 1: Getting Started with Households (5 minutes)

### Learning Objectives
- Understand what households are and why they're useful
- Create your first household
- Understand invite codes

### Script Outline

**Intro (30 seconds)**
> "Welcome to PetForce! In this video, you'll learn how to create your first household so you can share pet care with your family, roommates, or pet sitters."

**What is a Household? (1 minute)**
- Definition: A household is a group of people who help care for your pets
- Who should be in a household?
  - Family members
  - Roommates
  - Regular pet sitters
  - Dog walkers
  - Anyone who needs access to pet info

**Creating Your First Household (2 minutes)**
- Step 1: Navigate to Households tab
- Step 2: Tap "Create Household"
- Step 3: Enter household name (e.g., "The Buddy Family")
- Step 4: Add description (optional)
- Step 5: Review your invite code
- Step 6: Share with your first member

**Understanding Invite Codes (1 minute)**
- What they are: Unique codes to join your household
- Format: ABC-word-word (e.g., ABC-happy-puppy)
- They never expire (unless you regenerate)
- Keep them private

**Next Steps (30 seconds)**
> "Great! You've created your first household. Next, watch 'Inviting Members' to learn how to add people to your household."

### Visual Aids
- Animated diagram of household concept
- Screen recording of creation flow
- Highlight key UI elements

## Video 3: Managing Household Members (7 minutes)

### Learning Objectives
- View and manage member list
- Understand member roles
- Remove members safely
- Transfer leadership

### Script Outline

**Intro (30 seconds)**
> "Learn how to manage your household members, assign roles, and keep your household organized."

**Viewing Your Member List (1 minute)**
- Where to find it
- Understanding the member card
- Role badges (Leader, Member, Temporary)
- Last active timestamps

**Member Roles Explained (2 minutes)**
- **Leader**: Full control (that's you!)
  - Can approve/reject requests
  - Can remove members
  - Can regenerate invite code
  - Can transfer leadership

- **Member**: Regular access
  - Can view all household info
  - Can participate in pet care
  - Cannot manage other members

- **Temporary**: Limited-time access
  - Same as Member
  - Automatically expires
  - Great for pet sitters

**Removing a Member (1 minute)**
- When and why to remove
- Step-by-step: Tap member → Remove → Confirm
- What happens when removed
- They can always request to rejoin

**Transferring Leadership (1.5 minutes)**
- When to transfer (moving, primary caregiver change)
- Step-by-step: Member menu → Transfer Leadership
- Confirmation process
- What happens to your access (you stay as regular member)

**Adding Temporary Members (1 minute)** ⭐ UNIQUE FEATURE
- What are temporary members
- When to use (pet sitters, dog walkers, vacation help)
- How to add (same as regular + set expiration)
- Automatic removal after expiration
- Extending access if needed

**Next Steps (30 seconds)**
> "Your household is running smoothly! Next, learn about security and privacy."

### Visual Aids
- Member role comparison table
- Flowchart: When to remove vs. transfer
- Calendar showing temporary member expiration

## Video 4: Security & Privacy Best Practices (5 minutes)

### Learning Objectives
- Understand household security
- Learn when to regenerate codes
- Protect your pet's information

### Script Outline

**Intro (30 seconds)**
> "Keep your household secure and your pet's information private."

**Why Security Matters (1 minute)**
- Your household has access to:
  - Pet medical records
  - Vet contact info
  - Your home address
  - Emergency contacts
- Only invite people you trust

**When to Regenerate Your Invite Code (2 minutes)**
- Shared code publicly by accident
- Former member removed
- Suspicious activity
- Regular rotation (quarterly recommended)
- How to regenerate: Settings → Get New Code
- ⚠️ Warning: Old code stops working immediately

**Recognizing Suspicious Join Requests (1 minute)**
- Unknown email addresses
- Requests from people you didn't invite
- Multiple failed join attempts
- What to do: Reject + regenerate code

**Privacy Settings (30 seconds)**
- Who can see what in your household
- How to control pet information sharing
- Managing visibility of sensitive info

**Next Steps (30 seconds)**
> "Your household is secure! Now let's handle common issues."

### Visual Aids

## Video 5: Troubleshooting Common Issues (5 minutes)

### Learning Objectives
- Solve common household problems
- Know when to contact support
- Self-service solutions

### Script Outline

**Intro (30 seconds)**
> "Running into issues? Here are solutions to the most common household problems."

**Issue 1: "Invalid Invite Code" Error (1 minute)**
- Causes:
  - Code was regenerated
  - Typo in code entry
  - Code from wrong household
- Solutions:
  - Double-check code format
  - Ask sender for new code
  - Try QR code instead

**Issue 2: Join Request Stuck on "Pending" (1 minute)**
- Causes:
  - Leader hasn't checked requests yet
  - Leader notifications turned off
- Solutions:
  - Contact household leader directly
  - Leader: Check notifications tab
  - Wait 24 hours, then reach out to support

**Issue 3: Can't Remove a Member (1 minute)**
- Causes:
  - Only leaders can remove members
  - Trying to remove yourself (use "Leave Household")
- Solutions:
  - Ask current leader to remove
  - Transfer leadership to yourself first

**Issue 4: Lost Leadership (1 minute)**
- Causes:
  - Leadership was transferred
  - Account was merged
- Solutions:
  - Ask current leader to transfer back
  - Contact support for assistance

**Getting Help (30 seconds)**
- In-app support: Settings → Help
- Email: support@petforce.com
- Help center: help.petforce.com
- Community forums

**Next Steps (30 seconds)**
> "Still stuck? Our support team is here to help 24/7!"

### Visual Aids
- Error message screenshots
- Troubleshooting flowchart
- Support contact options

## Production Notes

### Style Guide
- **Tone**: Friendly, patient, encouraging
- **Pace**: Slow enough to follow, fast enough to stay engaged
- **Visuals**: Clean screen recordings, minimal text overlays
- **Music**: Soft, upbeat background music (low volume)
- **Captions**: Always include accurate captions

### Technical Specs
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (TikTok/Shorts version)
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Max File Size**: 500MB per video
- **Thumbnail**: Custom thumbnail for each video

### Recording Setup
- Screen recording: macOS (QuickTime) or Windows (OBS)
- Voiceover: Professional mic (Blue Yeti or similar)
- Editing: Adobe Premiere Pro or Final Cut Pro
- Captions: Auto-generated + manual review
