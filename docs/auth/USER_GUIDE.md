## Creating Your Account

### How to Sign Up

1. **Go to PetForce** (web or mobile app)
2. Click **"Create Account"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your information:
   - Email address
   - Password (see requirements below)
   - First and last name (optional)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for a verification link

### Password Requirements

Your password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)

**Example good passwords:**
- `MyDog2026!`
- `PetForce123`
- `Fluffy2024`

**Tip:** Use a password manager to create and store strong passwords securely.

### What Happens After Sign Up?

After you create your account:

1. **Email Sent**
   - We'll send a verification email to your address
   - This usually arrives within a few minutes

2. **Check Your Inbox**
   - Look for an email from PetForce
   - Check your spam folder if you don't see it

3. **Click the Link**
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - This confirms you own the email address

4. **Sign In**
   - After verifying, go back to PetForce
   - Sign in with your email and password
   - You're all set!

## Verifying Your Email

### Why Do I Need to Verify My Email?

Email verification helps us:
- Confirm you own the email address
- Protect your account from unauthorized access
- Send you important updates about your pets

### How to Verify Your Email

**Step 1: Find the Verification Email**

Look for an email with the subject:
> "Confirm your PetForce email"

**Step 2: Click the Verification Link**

Click the button or link in the email that says:
> "Confirm your email"

**Step 3: You're Verified!**

You'll see a confirmation message. Now you can sign in.

### Didn't Receive the Verification Email?

**Check Your Spam Folder**

Verification emails sometimes end up in spam or junk folders.

**Wait a Few Minutes**

Emails can take up to 5 minutes to arrive. Be patient.

**Resend the Verification Email**

If you still don't see it:

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Try to sign in with your email and password
3. You'll see a message: "Please verify your email"
4. Click **"Resend verification email"**
5. Check your inbox again (including spam)

**Note:** You can only request a new email every 5 minutes.

### Verification Link Expired

Verification links expire after 24 hours for security.

**What to Do:**

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Try to sign in
3. Click **"Resend verification email"**
4. Check for the new email
5. Click the new verification link

### Used an Old Verification Link

If you requested multiple verification emails, only the most recent link will work.

**Solution:** Always use the newest verification email.

## Signing In

### How to Sign In

1. Go to PetForce (web or mobile app)
2. Click **"Sign In"** or **"Log In"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Enter your password
5. Click **"Sign In"**

### Sign-In Methods

**Email and Password:**
- The email you registered with
- Your password

**Google Sign-In:**
- Click "Continue with Google"
- Choose your Google account
- Approve access

**Apple Sign-In:**
- Click "Continue with Apple"
- Approve with Face ID or Touch ID

**Mobile Biometrics (Face ID / Fingerprint):**
- Available after first sign-in
- Quick and secure access

### Stay Signed In

On trusted devices, you'll stay signed in for up to 90 days.

**Tips:**
- Only stay signed in on your personal devices
- Always sign out on shared computers
- Use biometric sign-in on your phone for convenience

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In - "Please Verify Your Email"

**What This Means:**

You created an account but haven't verified your email address yet.

**How to Fix:**

1. Check your email inbox for the verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. Try signing in again

If you can't find the email:
1. Click **"Resend verification email"** on the sign-in page
2. Wait a few minutes
3. Check your inbox and spam folder

---

### Can't Sign In - "Email or Password Incorrect"

**What This Means:**

The email or password you entered doesn't match our records.

**How to Fix:**

1. **Check your email address:**
   - Make sure there are no typos
   - Check if you used a different email

2. **Check your password:**
   - Make sure Caps Lock is off
   - Check for extra spaces
   - Try copying/pasting if you use a password manager

3. **Reset your password:**
   - Click **"Forgot Password?"**
   - Follow the password reset steps below

---

### Forgot Your Password

**How to Reset Your Password:**

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
5. Check your email for a password reset link
6. Click the link in the email
7. Enter your new password
8. Click **"Reset Password"**
9. Sign in with your new password

**Notes:**
- Reset links expire after 1 hour
- If the link expires, request a new one
- Check your spam folder if you don't see the email

---

### Not Receiving Emails from PetForce

**Check These Things:**

1. **Spam/Junk Folder**
   - Our emails sometimes go to spam
   - Mark PetForce as "Not Spam" to receive future emails

2. **Email Address**
   - Verify you're checking the right email
   - Make sure the email you registered with is correct

3. **Email Filters**
   - Check if you have filters blocking emails from `@petforce.app`

4. **Wait a Few Minutes**
   - Emails can take up to 5 minutes to arrive

5. **Try Again**
   - Request another verification or reset email
   - You can request one every 5 minutes

**Still Not Working?**

Contact our support team (see below). Include:
- The email address you're trying to verify
- When you created your account
- What emails you've tried to receive (verification, password reset, etc.)

---

### Account Locked After Too Many Failed Sign-Ins

**What This Means:**

For security, accounts are temporarily locked after several failed sign-in attempts.

**How to Fix:**

1. **Wait 15 Minutes**
   - The lock automatically expires after 15 minutes
   - This prevents unauthorized access attempts

2. **Reset Your Password**
   - Click "Forgot Password?"
   - This will unlock your account

3. **Try Again**
   - Sign in with the correct password
   - Be careful entering your password

**Prevention Tips:**
- Use a password manager to avoid typos
- Reset your password if you can't remember it
- Don't share your password with anyone

---

### Email Changed But Can't Sign In

**What Happened:**

If you changed your email, you need to verify the new email before signing in.

**How to Fix:**

1. Check the new email inbox
2. Look for a verification email
3. Click the verification link
4. Try signing in with the new email

**Note:** Until you verify the new email, you can still sign in with your old email.

---

### Signed Out Unexpectedly

**Why This Happens:**

You may be signed out if:
- Your session expired (after 90 days of inactivity)
- You changed your password
- You signed out on another device
- There was a security concern

**How to Fix:**

Simply sign in again with your email and password.

**Stay Signed In Longer:**
- Use the app regularly
- Enable biometric sign-in on mobile
- Don't clear your browser cookies

---

### Can't Access Account - Email No Longer Exists

**If You No Longer Have Access to Your Email:**

Unfortunately, you cannot recover an account without access to the registered email address.

**What to Do:**

1. **Create a New Account**
   - Use your current email address
   - Start fresh with a new account

2. **Contact Support**
   - We may be able to help if you can verify your identity
   - Email: support@petforce.app
   - Include: Account details, when you created it, last known access

**Prevention:**
- Keep your email address up to date
- Use an email you'll have long-term access to
- Update your email in account settings if it changes

## Account Security

### Keeping Your Account Safe

**Use a Strong Password:**
- At least 8 characters
- Mix of uppercase, lowercase, and numbers
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites

**Verify Your Email:**
- Adds an extra layer of security
- Required before you can sign in

**Don't Share Your Password:**
- PetForce will never ask for your password
- Don't share it with anyone, even support staff

**Sign Out on Shared Devices:**
- Always sign out when using someone else's device
- Don't stay signed in on public computers

**Enable Biometric Sign-In (Mobile):**
- Use Face ID or fingerprint
- More secure than typing a password
- Faster and more convenient

### Recognizing Phishing Emails

**Real PetForce Emails Come From:**
- `@petforce.app`
- `noreply@petforce.app`

**We Will Never:**
- Ask for your password by email
- Ask you to "verify" by entering your password
- Send suspicious links that don't go to petforce.app

**If You Receive a Suspicious Email:**
1. Don't click any links
2. Don't enter your password
3. Forward it to security@petforce.app
4. Delete the email

### What to Do If Your Account Is Compromised

**If You Think Someone Accessed Your Account:**

1. **Change Your Password Immediately**
   - Go to account settings
   - Or use "Forgot Password" from sign-in page

2. **Check Your Account Activity**
   - Review recent changes to your account
   - Look for unfamiliar activity

3. **Contact Support**
   - Email: security@petforce.app
   - We'll help secure your account

## Contact Support

### When to Contact Us

Contact our support team if:
- You can't verify your email after trying all troubleshooting steps
- You're not receiving any emails from PetForce
- You can't access your account and need help
- You believe your account was compromised
- You have questions not answered in this guide

### How to Reach Us

**Email Support:**
- support@petforce.app
- We respond within 24 hours (usually faster!)

**Include This Information:**
- Your email address (registered with PetForce)
- What you're trying to do
- What error message you see (if any)
- What troubleshooting steps you've already tried

**Security Issues:**
- security@petforce.app
- For suspected account compromise or security concerns

### What to Expect

**Response Time:**
- Most inquiries: Within 24 hours
- Security issues: Within 4 hours
- Weekends: May take longer

**We'll Help You:**
- Troubleshoot sign-in issues
- Verify your identity safely
- Reset your account access
- Answer questions about your account

| "Invalid or expired link" | Verification link too old | Request a new verification email |
