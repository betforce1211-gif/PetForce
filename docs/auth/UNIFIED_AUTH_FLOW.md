## Creating Your Account

PetForce uses a simple, secure process to create your account and keep your pet's information safe.

### Step 1: Visit the Sign Up Page

1. Go to the PetForce app at `/auth`
2. Click on the **Sign Up** tab at the top of the form

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

You'll see a form with these fields:

- **Email address**: Use an email you check regularly
- **Password**: Create a strong password (see [Password Requirements](#password-requirements))
- **Confirm password**: Type your password again to make sure it matches

**What makes a good password?**
- At least 8 characters
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- At least one number
- At least one special character (like !, @, #, $)

As you type your password, you'll see a strength indicator showing how secure it is.

### Step 3: Choose How to Sign Up

You have two options:

**Option 1: Sign up with Google or Apple**
- Click the **Continue with Google** or **Continue with Apple** button
- Follow the prompts to authorize PetForce
- You'll be signed in immediately (no email verification needed)

**Option 2: Sign up with email and password**
- Fill in all the fields
- Click **Create account**
- Check your email for a verification link

### Step 4: Verify Your Email

After creating your account with email and password:

1. Check your email inbox for a message from PetForce
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. You'll be redirected back to PetForce with a confirmation message
4. Now you can sign in

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam or junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (emails can take 2-5 minutes to arrive)
- Click "Resend verification email" on the sign-in page

## Signing In

Once your account is created and verified, signing in is simple.

### Step 1: Go to the Sign In Tab

1. Visit the PetForce app at `/auth`
2. The **Sign In** tab is selected by default

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

- **Email address**: The email you used to create your account
- **Password**: Your password

### Step 3: Sign In

Click **Sign in** and you'll be taken to your dashboard.

**Forgot your password?**
- Click **Forgot password?** below the password field
- Enter your email address
- Check your email for password reset instructions
- Create a new password
- Sign in with your new password

## Email Verification

Email verification helps keep your account secure and ensures we can reach you about your pets.

### Why We Verify Emails

- Protects your account from unauthorized access
- Ensures you receive important notifications about your pets
- Confirms you have access to the email address you provided

### Verification Process

1. **After registering**: You'll see a message: "Check your email to verify your account"
2. **In your inbox**: Look for an email with the subject "Verify your email address"
3. **Click the link**: This confirms your email address
4. **Verification complete**: You'll see a success message and can sign in

### Email Not Verified Yet?

If you try to sign in before verifying your email, you'll see this message:

> "Please verify your email address before logging in. Check your inbox for the verification link."

**What to do:**
1. Check your email inbox and spam folder
2. Click the verification link
3. Try signing in again

**Still can't find the email?**
- Click **Resend verification email** (you can do this once every 5 minutes)
- Add support@petforce.app to your contacts to prevent future emails from going to spam
- Contact our support team if you still don't receive it

## Common Issues

### "This email is already registered"

You see this error when trying to sign up with an email that already has an account.

**What this means:**
You already have a PetForce account with this email address.

**What to do:**
1. Click **Sign in** in the error message to switch to the Sign In tab
2. Enter your email and password
3. If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password?**

### "The email or password you entered is incorrect"

You see this error when signing in with incorrect credentials.

**What to do:**
1. Double-check your email address for typos
2. Make sure your password is correct (remember, passwords are case-sensitive)
3. If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password?**
4. If you're sure your credentials are correct, you may need to verify your email first

### "Please verify your email address before logging in"

You see this message when your email isn't verified yet.

**What to do:**
1. Check your email inbox for the verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. Try signing in again
4. If you can't find the email, click **Resend verification email**

### "Passwords don't match"

You see this error when signing up if your password and confirmation don't match.

**What to do:**
1. Make sure both password fields have exactly the same text
2. Check for extra spaces at the beginning or end
3. Remember that passwords are case-sensitive

### "You have made too many requests"

You see this error if you try to resend verification emails too frequently.

**What to do:**
1. Wait 5 minutes before trying again
2. Check your spam folder in case the email is already there
3. Contact support if you continue having issues

## Password Requirements

To keep your account secure, passwords must meet these requirements:

- **Minimum length**: 8 characters
- **At least one uppercase letter**: A-Z
- **At least one lowercase letter**: a-z
- **At least one number**: 0-9

**Password strength indicator:**
As you type your password, you'll see one of these labels:
- **Weak**: Your password needs improvement
- **Fair**: Your password is okay but could be stronger
- **Good**: Your password is strong
- **Strong**: Your password is very secure

**Tips for creating a strong password:**
- Use a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters
- Avoid common words or phrases
- Don't use personal information (birthdays, pet names, etc.)
- Use a different password for each website
- Consider using a password manager

## Privacy and Security

We take your privacy seriously:

- **Your password is encrypted**: We never store your password in plain text
- **Secure connections**: All communication is encrypted with HTTPS
- **Email verification**: Confirms you own the email address
- **Account recovery**: Password reset requires access to your email
