## Q2 2026 (Apr-Jun) ðŸ“‹ PLANNED

### Multi-Household Support
**Problem**: Pet sitters manage multiple households
**Solution**: Switch between households in app

**Features**:
- Household switcher UI
- "Default household" preference
- Quick household search
- Recent households list

**Success Metrics**:
- 15% of users in 2+ households
- <2 taps to switch households

### Advanced Roles
**Problem**: Need more granular permissions
**Solution**: Expand beyond leader/member

**New Roles**:
- **Admin**: Can manage members, settings (not transfer leadership)
- **Caregiver**: Full pet care access, no household management
- **Viewer**: Read-only access (grandparents, emergency contacts)
- **Pet Sitter**: Temporary caregiver with time-limited access

**Success Metrics**:
- 30% of households use custom roles
- <10 support tickets about permissions

### Household-Level Settings
**Problem**: Settings are user-level, not household-level
**Solution**: Household preferences

**Settings**:
- Timezone (for medication reminders)
- Notification preferences (per-household)
- Privacy settings (public vs private profile)
- Household avatar/photo

**Success Metrics**:
- 60% of households customize settings
- 25% upload household photo

### Activity Feed
**Problem**: Family members don't know what others did
**Solution**: Household activity timeline

**Features**:
- "Who fed Fluffy today?"
- "When was the last vet visit?"
- Filter by pet, member, activity type
- Search activity history

**Success Metrics**:
- 70% of households view feed weekly
- 40% use search/filters

### Household Notes & Calendar
**Problem**: No shared note-taking or scheduling
**Solution**: Collaborative notes + calendar

**Features**:
- Shared notes (vet instructions, feeding schedule)
- Household calendar (vet appointments, grooming)
- Reminders (medication, feeding)
- Calendar sync (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar)

**Success Metrics**:
- 50% of households create notes
- 35% add calendar events

**Q2 Launch Date**: June 15, 2026

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk**: Performance degrades with scale
- **Mitigation**: Load testing, database optimization, caching

### Product Risks
- **Risk**: Users don't need multi-household
- **Mitigation**: User research, beta testing, phased rollout

### Competitive Risks
- **Risk**: Rover/Wag copies our features
- **Mitigation**: Patent invite code format, maintain 2-year lead

### Operational Risks
- **Risk**: Support team overwhelmed
- **Mitigation**: Self-service help docs, in-app tutorials, chatbot
